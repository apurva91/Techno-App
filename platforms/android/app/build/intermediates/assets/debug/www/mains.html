About Mains Structure

It is the second round of Technothlon which is conducted during Techniche - the annual techno-management festival of IIT Guwahati. The top 50 teams from each squad compete against each other in challenging events designed to test their innovation, creativity and imagination. Exciting prizes await for the teams who prove their mettle by ‘out-thinking’ the other competitors.
